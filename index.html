South African Wildlife

The wildlife of South Africa and their habitats

Elephant Fun Facts

  • Elephants are the world's largest land animal
  • There are 2 types of elephants in South Africa
  • Elephants use dirt as sunscreen
  • Elephants are pregnant for 22 months

Lion Fun Facts

  • There are approximately 13,000 lions in South Africa
    1. 2,300 are wild
    2. The rest are in game reserves and in captivity
  • Lions usually live in groups of 10 to 15 animals
    1. These are called prides
  • A lion may sleep up to 20 hours in a day
  • Lions are the second largest cat on the planet

African Penguin Fun Facts

  • African penguins are monogamous
    1. This means that they mate with the same individual for many years
  • They can hold their breath for over 2 minutes
  • They can dive 400 feet deep
  • African Penguins are the only breed of penguin found in Africa
